About us

This is a traditional restraurant that was founded
in 1998 by Antony and Bros.Most of our foods have
cultural backgrounds and are meant to bring back the taste of our
traditional foods.The foods are created from traditional recipies
that are so famous in Kenya and its environs.

Try out our most loved delicacies:

  1. Dressed Chicken with ugali (Kuku wa nazi)
  2. Grilled beef with vegetables
  3. New pilau masala with roasted chicken breast
  4. Fish fingers
  5. Fried chicken with fries
  6. Ram ribs with Fries
